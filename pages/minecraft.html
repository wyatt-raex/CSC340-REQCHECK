Minecraft (Java Edition)

Summary

The World Is Yours For The Making
Prepare for an adventure of limitless possibilities as you build, mine, battle mobs, and explore the ever-changing Minecraft landscape.

Requirements

Minimum Requirements

  • CPU: Intel Core i3-3210 3.2 GHz/ AMD A8-7600 APU 3.1 GHz or equivalent
  • GPU: Nvidia GeForce 400 Series or AMD Radeon HD 7000 series with OpenGL 4.4
  • RAM: 4GB
  • Storage: At least 1GB for game core, maps and other files

Recommended Requirements

  • CPU: Intel Core i5-4690 3.5GHz / AMD A10-7800 APU 3.5 GHz or equivalent
  • GPU: GeForce 700 Series or AMD Radeon Rx 200 Series (excluding integrated chipsets) with OpenGL 4.5
  • RAM: 8GB
  • Storage: 4GB (SSD is recommended)
